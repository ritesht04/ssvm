Prarthana

Prarthana, a timeless act of devotion, is the sacred bridge that connects our hearts to the divine. Through this heartfelt prayer, words transform into whispers of the soul, and silence blossoms into a symphony of unwavering faith and profound reverence.

Artistic Activities

Our artistic activities foster imagination and self-expression, transforming classrooms into vibrant studios where students explore their talents, develop their skills, and bring their unique visions to life.

Cultural Activities

Our cultural activities enrich the school experience by honoring traditions and promoting global awareness. Through festivals, performances, and interactive events, students gain a deep appreciation for various cultures, fostering unity and respect in our vibrant community.

Exploring New Horizons

Under the guidance and support of our dedicated faculty, a talented student from our school has created a detailed model of Chandrayaan 3. This impressive project exemplifies our commitment to providing robust scientific knowledge and supervision, nurturing curiosity, innovation, and a passion for space exploration among our students.

Empowering Futures

Our Naari Shiksha sessions are dedicated to women empowerment, educating our students about gender equality and the importance of women's roles in society. Through interactive workshops, inspiring talks, and engaging activities, we foster an environment of respect and support, encouraging all students to champion the cause of women's empowerment and contribute to a more equitable world.

Unleashing Digital Creativity

Our computer lab is a dynamic space where students embark on journeys of technological discovery and innovation. Equipped with state-of-the-art hardware and software, our lab fosters digital literacy and creativity. From coding challenges to multimedia projects, students explore the limitless possibilities of technology under the guidance of our skilled instructors, preparing them for success in the digital age.

From Local to Global

Our students compete and excel at block, state, and international levels. They showcase their skills, teamwork, and determination, representing our school with pride. These experiences teach them valuable lessons in resilience and sportsmanship, proving that with passion, anything is achievable.

इस प्रकार की राष्ट्रीय शिक्षा-प्रणाली का विकास करना है जिसके द्वारा ऐसी युवा-पीढ़ी का निर्माण हो सके जो हिन्दुत्वनिष्ठ एवं राष्ट्रभक्ति से ओत-प्रोत हो, शारीरिक, प्राणिक, मानसिक, बौद्धिक एवं आध्यात्मिक दृष्टि से पूर्ण विकसित हो तथा जो जीवन की वर्तमान चुनौतियों का सामना सफलता पूर्वक कर सकें और उसका जीवन ग्रामों, वनों, गिरिकन्दराओं एवं झुग्गी-झोपड़ियों में निवास करने वाले दीन-दुःखी अभावग्रस्त अपने बान्धवों को सामाजिक कुरीतियों, शोषण एवं अन्याय से मुक्त कराकर राष्ट्र जीवन को समरस, सुसम्पन्न एवं सुसंस्कृत बनाने के लिए समर्पित हो।
